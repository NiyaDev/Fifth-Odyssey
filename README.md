
Many years have passed since the days of Rome, golden ages came and dark times went. Through it all we have endured.
But now the Empire is surrounded by enemies, The Serbs to the northwest, Bulgars to the northwest, the Frankish crusaders to the south and the Turks to the east. But worst of all isn't the foreign threats, its the domestic ones. The Empire is curropt to it's core and the succession of the crown is contested to say the least.

XXX, a nobleman with a passion for the stories and knowledge of his ancestors finds something new within a newly purchased store; Works of the philosopher Plato. He quickly gets to work translating the texts and one of the first discoveries he made was Plato's writing about an ancient city beyond the Pillars of Hercules that was sunk by the gods. He knows better than to believe in parables, but every story has a hint of truth and it makes him wonder about what could be out there. He cross references old roman ship manifests and other books and legends and comes up with a theory; The world is larger than the ancients believed, and there might be land yet claimed.
He begins working himself to the bone making calculations and writing treatises. He builds his families wealth slowly to fund his mission and in a few days he will show his work to the emperor himself!

And then on March 31st, 1337 he passed away in his sleep.
His wills were clear; his Daughter XX will handle the estate while his Sons XX and XX will given the family business and the accumulated wealth respectively. (DAUGHTER) found her father's work and brought it to her siblings and they all agreed to carry out their father's mission; To save the children of Rome by making a voyage across the sea to a land unknown to the world named for the story their father loved the most.

The Fifth Odyssey


### Features
- Illysia:
  - Start as an interesting take on Building-based countries, recruiting people from across the Greek world to join the voyage.
  - Use the Fifth Odyssey situation to view your progress, pay for ships to protect your expedition, and finally leave the old world behind.
  - 30+ missions during the situation
  - XX+ missions on landing
  - Optional missions to help guide your progress.
  - Reworked new world areas to better facilitate colonization.
